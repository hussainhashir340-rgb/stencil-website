How an order gets made

From your file to a folded shirt, four stages.

Every order moves through the same shop floor, in the same order, whether it's one shirt or two hundred.

01 — DESIGN

You send the art

Upload a design or describe what you want. We check resolution and colors before anything gets cut.

02 — STENCIL

We cut the screen

Your art is burned onto a mesh screen — one screen per color, by hand, in-house.

03 — PRESS

Ink meets fabric

Each shirt is loaded onto the press and pulled by hand, then cured so the ink won't crack or fade.

04 — SHIP

Folded and out the door

Quality-checked, folded, and shipped or ready for pickup — tracking sent the moment it leaves the shop.
